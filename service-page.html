LCD Projector

An LCD projector uses liquid crystals to create images, filtering light through colored panels. This produces vibrant colors on screens or surfaces, offering a portable and affordable display solution

LED Projector

Tiny lights shine directly, offering energy efficiency, long lifespan, and compact design. Vibrant colors but potential for lower contrast compared to LCD.

DLP Projecor

Tiny mirrors reflect light, delivering high contrast and deep blacks. Compact and energy-efficient, but may have slightly cooler color temperature.
